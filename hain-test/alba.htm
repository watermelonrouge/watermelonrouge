Q1 EPS In Line

Bottom Line:

EDR adjusted Q1/17 EPS of $0.01 was in line with our estimates and slightly below consensus. Operating cash flow and FCF came in ahead of our expectation at ~$10M and $0.3M, respectively. Capital expenditures were light on the quarter and we expect spending to rise through the remained of 2017 to meet guidance.

Key Points

EDR reported Q1/17 headline EPS $0.05, after adjusting for foreign exchange gains adj. EPS came in at $0.01, in line our estimate of $0.01 and slightly below consensus at $0.02.

EDR pre-announced Q1/17 production of 1.1Moz silver and 11.7koz gold. Silver by-product cash costs and AISC for Q1/17 came in at $7.81/oz and $18.24/oz, respectively, above FY2017 guidance. The company made no changes to its 2017 guidance of 5.2-5.7Moz silver and 50-53koz gold at by-product AISC of $14.00-15.00/oz.

Q1/17 reported operating cash flow and FCF of ~$10M and $0.3M, above our expectations of $7M and negative $11M, respectively.

Given the company has only spent 12% of planned capital expenditures, we expect spending to increase through he remainder of 2017.

EDR exited the quarter with $71M in cash and $6.5M in short-term debt.

Our Thesis

EDR is a "transition story," with plans to increase production by 50% by 2019E through the development of Terronera. AISC are expected to decline 30% over the same time frame.

Valuation

Our valuation is based on a 2017E payable production forecast of 5.4Moz silver and 54koz gold at co-product all-in sustaining cost of US$21.44/oz silver equivalent. The target price is based on a 50/50 weighted blend of 1.7x P/NPV adjusted for market capitalization and 10.0x P/CF adjusted for growth.

Upside Scenario

3.0x NAV multiple at BMO prices ($20/oz long term).

Downside Scenario

1.0x NAV multiple. Spot silver price declines back to $15/oz level and sector multiples collapse.

Company Description

Endeavour Silver operates three mines located in historical silver districts in Mexico. The company is focused on evaluating and developing its greenfields pipeline of the prospective El Compas, Terronera and Parral projects.
