  • We Are Breaker

    As a collective of artists, visionaries, and fans, we understand the frustrations surrounding the entertainment industry business model. It’s tired, it’s slow, and it displaces ownership. Breaker was created to change all of that with a new, transparent way for creators to distribute their work and a place where fans can access a more diverse collection of creative work.

  • Transparency is Key

    When you remove the gatekeepers who own your intellectual property (IP), who keep you from performance metrics and developmental research, the result is a nimbler, more knowledgeable, business-savvy creator who gets paid directly in real-time for each transaction.

  • Powered by Pioneers

    Breaker was built to create a deeper connection between artists and fans. This means no intermediaries, no middlemen just a clean peer to peer transaction. It’s a fairer, more transparent monetization model made possible through blockchain technology. Designed to create deeper connections between fans and artists.

  • More Artists, More Authenticity

    Breaker is a global distribution platform created to empower artists all over the world. No limitations or barriers. Upload your film, music, podcast, and art for all to see, everywhere, no restrictions.

  • Your Data is Your Data

    The data on Breaker is used to personalize your experience and help creators understand your reaction to their work in order to develop projects you enjoy and they want to create. But most importantly, we will never sell data to a third party. Never.

  • Inspired by the Community

    We exist because of our community. Those who like us, saw that there was a better way forward. Who believe that Ethereum blockchain technology can evolutionize how business is conducted and how creativity is expressed and distributed. We will continue to be inspired by the dreamers, the believers, the rebels, the Breakers.
